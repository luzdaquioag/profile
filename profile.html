SAN JOSE AT CALGARY

Flames reach Stanley Cup Finals for first time in 15 years

CALGARY, Alberta -- The pandemonium began while the puck was still in play, sliding alone toward the tranquility of the San Jose Sharks' unguarded net.

When the final score of the Western Conference finals bisected the goal line as time expired, the Calgary Flames and their fans were going too crazy to notice. With one more fantastic finish, Canada's team had earned the right to play for the Cup. Captain Jarome Iginla scored his 10th playoff goal and the Flames advanced to their first Stanley Cup Finals in 15 years with a 3-1 victory over the Sharks in Game 6 Wednesday night.
